Details

Dracaena trifasciata is a species of flowering plant in the family Asparagaceae, native to tropical West Africa from Nigeria east to the Congo. It is most commonly known as the snake plant, Saint George's sword, mother-in-law's tongue, and viper's bowstring hemp, among other names. Snake plants are one of the easiest houseplants to keep and need very little maintenance once you find the perfect spot for them with lots of indirect sunlight. However, overwatering is the quickest way to kill it, so water this plant only when soil is dry.
